About

Digestif converts your photostream into an email digest. Your friends and family subscribe and decide how frequently they want digests delivered. That way, when you post new photographs your friends and family are notified on their terms.

How does this work?

You connect your Flickr or Instagram account to Digestif via the frontpage. Digestif generates a sign-up page you can share with your friends and family. Every day Digestif checks your photostream for new photographs. Digestif sends email digests of your photographs to your friends and family according to their preferred delivery rate.

Which photographs are sent?

All photographs that are marked public, friends, or family. Private photographs are never sent.

Why?

When our son, Henry, was born we wanted to share photographs with our friends and family. There are so many ways to share your photographs online but none of them are simple.

  • Facebook forces everyone to be a member of their walled garden.
  • Instagram. You have to pester your friends and family to download an app and register.
  • Flickr is great! But notification of new photographs is tricky.
  • Apple Photostream. Pretty good, if you all own Apple devices.

A lot of people resort to sending emails with attachments which is universal. But now you have to spam your friends and family. Grand-parents may want photos every day but your brother only wants to see photos once a month. Managing this is a pain. So we created Digestif.

Who is behind this?

James Clarke is building this during Henry's naps at the weekend.
